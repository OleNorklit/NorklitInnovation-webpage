What I do

I help companies that needs expert knowledge of product development. Especially for wireless communication or medical devices.

You might be an investor needing insight into the technology of a company before investing, or you might be a company that have a product idea, maybe even a few engineers to spare, but need someone with experience to lead and ensure a good start of the product creation.

Technology Business

Advisory within a product portfolio, its key technical diffentiators, important IP, and suggestions for potential new developments or applications.

Technology and Architecture

Advisory of product technology architectures, modularisation, system performance and suggestions how to optimize them.

Wireless Communication Technology

Consulting within wireless communications technology, especially the interplay between antennas, RF, and digital comunication. Open for temporary management roles.

Medical Device Development

Consulting in medical device development. How to organise system archetecture and design history files for fast deployment.

Ideas to Reality

Product Renewal
